About Us

"Get2Vietnam” is a website where you can spiritually get to know our country, Vietnam. We acknowledged that many people nowadays would love to expand their knowledge by getting to know other countries such as: cultures, speciality dishes, languages and places to travel. Therefore, we created this product in order to help you in a helpful way. Get2Vietnam contains major cities, in each cities you can watch videos about Vietnam. Moreover, you can learn many basic conversations through videos in order to communicate if you plan to travel to Vietnam.
