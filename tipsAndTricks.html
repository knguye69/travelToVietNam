Tips and Tricks

  1. Take your VISA seriously indeed
  2. Buy your train ticket directly from train station, not from hotels or agents
  3. Breakfast at hotel is often included, but often bad
  4. Be carefull with traffic in Vietnam. Traffic accidents are the number one cause of tourist death worldwide
  5. Vietnamese people are friendly, hopitable and sweet
  6. Vietnam is a food paradise
  7. Secure you belongings all the time
  8. Do not leave your phone on the table
  9. The traffic is hectic
  10. Vietnam is bigger than most people think
  11. Get off the "tourist trail", use tour as a plan B
  12. The beaches may disappoint you
  13. Be carefull with your card
  14. Everything you buy in Vietnam will be a bargain
  15. Tap water can not be drunk directly
  16. Ready to be hagged
  17. Weather varies a lot
  18. Dress appropriately
  19. Everyone is a "millionaire"
